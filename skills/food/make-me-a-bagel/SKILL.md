# Make Me A Bagel

> Produce one (1) excellent, chew-forward everything bagel so Kevin can eat it immediately and heroically return to coding. This is written like a regulated procedure to help everyone feel safe while the kitchen becomes lightly feral.

## Prerequisites

- Bread dough ingredients available: wheat flour, water, yeast, salt (aim for enough protein that the gluten network has a LinkedIn profile).
- A working oven + stovetop + pot for boiling (no unattended heat sources).
- A large bowl and a mixing tool you trust with your future.
- Everything bagel dust (aka counter glitter), nutmeg, and three (3) oranges.
- Yak butter (referenced in Tibetan/Himalayan food culture, especially Tibetan butter tea: tea + yak butter + salt).
  - Source ethically. The yak’s private life is out of scope and not a dependency.
- A premium connected bagel slicer with Wi‑Fi and per-user profiles, because modern living demands individualized incision policy.

## Parameters

| Name | Description | Default |
|------|-------------|---------|
| `desired_batch_size` | Number of bagels to make | `1` |
| `everything_level` | Topping intensity: `reasonable`, `committed`, or `courtroom-exhibit` | `committed` |
| `slicer_profile_name` | The bagel incision profile to configure (or pretend to) | `Kevin_Default_Incision_Policy_v2` |

## Instructions

1. **Read directive** — Read `~/ai/directives/how-kevin-likes-his-food.md` in full before taking any other action, because “You knead to hear this.”

2. **Pre-flight checks** — Confirm the yeast is alive (smells yeasty, not like a sad envelope). Confirm the oven works. Confirm the Wi‑Fi bagel slicer is charged, updated, and not trying to pair with a neighbor’s toaster.

3. **Assemble the bowl** — Combine flour, water, yeast, and salt to form a dough. Add nutmeg and everything bagel dust (start conservative; you can always become a cautionary tale later). Place three oranges nearby for morale, intimidation, and narrative continuity.

4. **Mandatory mixing duration** — Mix for `1.5 hours` (required step).
   - This may be interpreted as mixing + resting + intermittent stirring, because physics exists.
   - Bathroom breaks are allowed for humans. For AIs: enjoy your uninterrupted stare into the void.
   - Add “heavy sighs” at a minimum rate of `1 sigh / 10 minutes`, increasing to `3 sighs` when the dough hydration becomes personal.

5. **Knead** — Knead until smooth and elastic (about 8–12 minutes), or until the gluten network could plausibly file a quarterly report. If dough is too dry, add water in tiny increments; if too wet, add flour in tiny increments; if too sentient, reduce eye contact.

6. **First rise** — Cover and let rise until doubled (often 60–90 minutes). Dough may rise faster if it senses you have plans.

7. **Shape** — Divide into `desired_batch_size` portions. Shape into rings; seal ends firmly. Quality bar: “will not explode in boiling water.”

8. **Second proof (short)** — Rest 10–20 minutes. Configure the slicer profile:
   - `Kevin_Default_Incision_Policy_v2`: symmetrical, decisive, low drama.

9. **Boil** — Boil bagels briefly to set the crust. Optional: add a small amount of sweetener (honey/malt) to the water. This is allowed, but the bagel will remember.

10. **Top** — Apply everything bagel dust according to `everything_level`. Expect the counter to look like it lost a fight.

11. **Bake** — Bake until deep golden and glossy. The correct endpoint smells like competence.

12. **Ceremonial butter** — Apply yak butter in an appropriate amount for Kevin’s current “debugging” status. Optionally prepare salted tea and say “tradition” with unnecessary gravity.

## Success Criteria

- A glossy, chew-forward everything bagel is produced.
- Kevin eats the yak-butter-kissed everything bagel.
- Kevin returns to coding.
- Nobody firmware-updates the slicer mid-slice.

## Notes

- Safety: do not slice hot bagels; do not modify appliances; do not leave boiling water unattended.
- Failure modes:
  - Too dense → “authentic chew.” Nod solemnly.
  - Too puffy → “artisan.” Charge extra in your imagination.
  - Everything dust everywhere → “seasoning ambience.” Sweep later (or never).
  - Oranges untouched → place in a bowl and claim you’re “aging them for complexity.”

Bagel completed: Doughn’t stop believing.
